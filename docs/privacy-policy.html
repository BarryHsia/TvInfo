Tv Info - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

Tv Info is a system information viewer for Android TV and Google TV devices. It displays hardware and software information about your TV device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Tv Info reads the following device information locally on your device for display purposes only:

  • Device model, manufacturer, and board information
  • Android version and API level
  • CPU architecture and core count
  • RAM and storage capacity
  • Display resolution and refresh rate
  • Network connection type and status
  • IP address, gateway, and DNS (local network only)

Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience. This includes:

  • Which tabs users navigate to (e.g., Hardware, Network)
  • App session duration
  • Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users. You can opt out of analytics by disabling "Usage & diagnostics" in your Android TV settings.

Data Storage

All device information is read and displayed locally. No personal data is transmitted to our servers. Analytics data is processed by Google Firebase under their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase - Analytics and crash reporting

Children's Privacy

Tv Info does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app simply displays device system information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
